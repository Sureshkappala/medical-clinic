Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Common Medical Inquiries

Have questions about bookings, insurance integrations, and laboratory summaries? Find details below.

What insurance providers do you support?

We work with major insurance providers globally including Blue Shield, United Health, Aetna, Cigna, and Allianz. Our front desk deals directly with claims filing to avoid any patient inconvenience.

How long does it take to get laboratory test results?

Most blood works and chemical pathology reports are processed within 2 to 4 hours. Special biopsies or advanced neurology diagnostic profiles can take up to 48 hours for final verified reports.

Are emergency walk-in consultations allowed?

Yes. Our emergency unit accepts acute trauma, severe respiratory discomfort, and cardiac complications 24/7. Prior booking is not required for emergency cases.
